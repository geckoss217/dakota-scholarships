How Scores & Winning Odds Work

A plain-English breakdown of the methodology behind every Match Score, Winning Odds estimate, and Tier Badge in the tracker.

Important: All scores and odds are educated estimates based on publicly available data and profile analysis, not verified acceptance rate data. Always confirm eligibility and deadlines directly with each organization.
Section 01

Match Score (0-100%)

The Match Score reflects how well Dakota's profile aligns with each scholarship's stated criteria, evaluated across five weighted dimensions:

DimensionWeightWhat's Evaluated
Field of Study Alignment30%How closely the scholarship focus matches Environmental Engineering / Water Sustainability
Academic Profile25%GPA, IB Diploma candidacy, and test scores relative to typical award recipients
Demographic / Eligibility Fit20%Whether Dakota meets specific criteria: women in STEM, Colorado resident, Native heritage, etc.
Extracurricular and Leadership15%How athletic achievements, volunteer work, and leadership roles align with stated criteria
School Specificity10%Bonus for scholarships tied to schools where Dakota is already accepted (Cal Poly, etc.)

Scores reflect application strength before essays and recommendations -- those are the multiplier you control.

Section 02

Winning Odds (%)

Winning Odds are estimated using a three-factor formula:

Winning Odds = (Awards Per Year / Estimated Applicant Pool) x Profile Strength Multiplier

Awards Per Year -- sourced from each org's published data or estimated from historical patterns.

Estimated Applicant Pool -- based on scope (national vs. regional vs. school-specific) and eligibility criteria.

Profile Strength Multiplier -- adjusts raw odds based on Match Score:

Match ScoreMultiplierEffect
90-100%1.5xStrong boost -- excellent fit
80-89%1.2xModerate boost -- good fit
70-79%1.0xNo adjustment -- average fit
60-69%0.8xSlight reduction -- partial fit
Below 60%0.6xSignificant reduction -- weak fit
Section 03

Tier badges are assigned based on the combined Match Score and Winning Odds:

Section 05

Assumptions and Limitations

  • No scholarship org publishes true acceptance rates. All odds are modeled estimates, not verified data.
  • Scores reflect Dakota's profile as of early 2026. Re-evaluate as new achievements are added.
  • Essays and recommendations matter enormously. A strong essay can significantly outperform the estimated odds.
  • Deadlines shift annually. Always verify on the official website before submitting.
  • Regional scholarships may have conservative odds estimates -- local pool sizes are harder to verify.
  • Scores reflect profile fit only and do not account for the quality of competing applicants in any given year.
